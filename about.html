Rio Fit Meals are flavorful, healthy, well-balanced meals delivered to your door and ready to eat in minutes. The special chef-inspired sous-vide method vacuum seals the flavor to keep it tasting the way the chef intended. Made-to-order and with love, Rio Fit Meals can be easily and quickly reheated by placing each pouch in boiling water or the microwave. Our Rio Fit Meals come in many flavorful dish combinations, with customized plans and are crafted by award-winning Executive Chef Rodrigo Souza, who takes pride in his dishes and his Michelin-Star styled execution and presentation. We offer weekly delivery service and make our Rio Fit Meals on Mondays. Our delivery area is extensive (from Worcester to Boston, and Milford, MA to southern NH).

Our Story

Owner and Executive Chef of Rio Fit Meals, Rodrigo Souza, loves to feed his family and friends with healthy meals made (with love) at his restaurant, Comeketo Brazilian Steakhouse. Rodrigo has dedicated his life to cooking flavorful and delicious meals for all – including those in need – by supporting his local community, even during tough times. Rodrigo’s Brazilian (Rio de Janeiro) culture, passion for healthy eating, and busy lifestyle inspired the idea and the Rio Fit Meals brand name. With on-the-go lifestyles, Rio Fit Meals saves time and money. When Rodrigo first learned about these pre-made prepared packaged meals that seal in flavor, his research included trying every available dish. Although the existing brands offered impressive packaging and easy online ordering, the bland unappetizing food was a huge disappointment. Immediately, Rodrigo knew he could provide better tasting, fresh and healthy meals using the same sous-vide packaging and preparation method. At that moment, Rio Fit Meals was born.
